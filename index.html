Magic

Level: Top left - do not include the decimal

Intelligence/Dexterity: Character screen under Attributes

Class Magic Damage: Class screen - the 6 classes this applies to are: Mage (+5), Adept (+8), Master Mage (+12), Great Wizard (+16), Divine Staff (+32) and Divine Sage (+64)

Staff Spell Damage: Character screen - hover over your staff. If you have a staff where the Spell Damage is similar to +946.80005 for example, you will only need to enter 946.8
